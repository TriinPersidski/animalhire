Meet our animals

CAMELS

Our camels are professionally trained and love the interaction with people. You can hire our camels for your next event and offer something special for your festival guests. Our camels enjoy going to Festivals, Fairs, Markets, Shows and even private parties.

CAMEL HIRE

We hire our camels and other animals for video production and advertising.

Make your Christmas Celebrations with Camels a memorable and entertaining event.

PONIES

Our ponies Barney, Monty & Justin take younger kids for a ride. They are adorable and gentle with kids.

We suggest pony riders be old enough to wear a helmet & sit up by themselves. Parents are welcome to walk alongside & assist young riders. Enclosed shoes must be worn to assist.

PONY HIRE

Ponies are available for large events and children's parties. We bring the ponies, handlers & ASA approved helmets to your party. A larger pony or horse can be included for older children.

PETTING ZOO

Billabong Ranch has a fantastic Mobile Animal Nursery. The larger animals roam around while the smaller animals are presented to the children to hold & pet by one of Billabong's Animal Nursery staff. The enclosure is adjustable & therefore can be made to fit most areas. Ponies of varying sizes are also available for pony rides.

PETTING ZOO HIRE

Petting Zoo is wonderful addition to any Christmas Party, Fete in fact any event that involves children.

You may have seen us out & about at Shepparton & Bendigo Market Places, Echuca Steam Rally, One & All Inclusion Fun Day & Safeway to name a few.

Why should you hire us?

We are professional mobile animal hire service.

All our animals love to travel and so do we! We are located in Echuca, Victoria and are flexible with locations and notice time. We come to in Victoria and Southen NSW. Our popular destinations are Bendigo, Shepparton, Melbourne and Surrounds, Geelong, Swan Hill, Ballarat, Albury and Torquay.

We offer flexible animal hire packages to suit your event size and selection of animals. We are available for day, weekend and even weeklong bookings.

Our packages are suitable for corporate functions, school fetes, birthdays and parties, weddings and religious ceremonies, festivals and shows and advertising and promotions.

Our animals are well looked after. Billabong Ranch is an accredited and fully insured business. We pride ourselves on reliability and the quality of our service.

We give our best to meet our customers wish list. It is rewarding to see how much joy our animals bring to everyone they meet.

Billabong Ranch won the Award of GMCU Allianz Campaspe Murray Business in Tourism category 2017.
