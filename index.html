CAMEL HIRE

Our camels are professionally trained and love the interaction with people. You can hire our camels for your next event and offer something special for your festival guests. Our camels enjoy going to Festivals, Fairs, Markets, Shows and even private parties.

Make your Christmas Celebrations with Camels a memorable and entertaining event.

PONY HIRE

Ponies are available for large events and children's parties.  
We bring the ponies, handlers & ASA approved helmets to your party. 
A larger pony or horse can be included for older children.

MOBILE PETTING ZOO HIRE

Our Petting Zoo is a Mobile Animal Farm. Smaller animals are presented to the children to
hold and pet by one of our Animal Nursery staff. 
Billabong Animal Nursery is always popular amongst the young & young at heart.
Our staff will set up the nursery, supervises at all times, clean up and load the animals for their journey home.
